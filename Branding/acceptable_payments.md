# 🎩 Acceptable Payments at JabberJuicy

Welcome, dear guest. At this place of buisness, currency is a matter of imagination, whimsy, and mild temporal instability. Please review the following accepted forms of payment before taking tea.

---

## 💳 Accepted Methods

### 🎩 Riddle Credit

Offer a riddle no one can solve. The longer it lingers unanswered, the larger your tab. Riddles that *sound* meaningful but collapse under inspection should go in the tip jar.

---

### 🕰️ Borrowed Time

Payment may be made in minutes, hours, or entire afternoons. Time is flexible here... though, once surrendered cannot be reclaimed.

---

### 🧵 Threads of Nonsense

A story spun with impeccable illogic, value rises with absurdity. Easily measured in stares of disbelief and clouds of confusion.

---

### 🎶 Song Fragments

Partial melodies, especially those that never resolve, are accepted. Repetition may increase worth, depending on irritation levels.

---

### 🍰 Unbirthday Tokens

You may pay with the joy of enthusiastic celebration of any of the 364 days that are not your birthday.

---

### 🫖 Tea Leaves Futures

Predictions made from tea leaves may be used as speculative currency. Accuracy is discouraged; honesty is rewarded.

---

## ⚠️ Notes

* Traditional currency (cash, card, etc.) may be politely ignored.
* Overpayment is encouraged but never acknowledged.
* Underpayment may result in additional squeeze, whether it's worth the juice or not.

---

Enjoy your stay, and remember:

> “Beware if it's not JabberJuicy!” ☕
